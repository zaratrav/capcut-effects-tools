About CapCut Effects & Transitions

CapCut effects and transitions enhance video storytelling by adding motion, style, and visual impact.

Transitions create smooth scene changes, while effects and filters define the overall aesthetic of your video.

Green screen and chroma key tools allow background replacement and advanced compositing.

Overlays and split screen enable multi-layer editing and creative compositions.

Text animation and keyframes provide control over motion and dynamic visuals.

Speed ramp, slow motion, and reverse effects add cinematic timing and storytelling depth.
